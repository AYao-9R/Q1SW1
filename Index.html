Why I love Bass

The bass guitar has always been one of my favorite hobby and instruments I enjoy doing. Started around 2021, the bass has been my "escape" from the whole pandemic situation, to relax and
just enjoy the moment. My love for music has alway been there even before I started playing the bass, trying out different intruments such as the drums, guitar, and chimes. I didn't really find
those instruments interesting enough though, because it didnt seem like me. But after discovering the bass, it made me feel like I could be good at something, which made "complete"" in a way.
I soon found myself playing the bass guitar in our church's worship ministry, giving back to the Lord by offering up my talents and giving my praise (see what I did there? hehe), giving back
the glory He deserves.

There are a lot of amazing bassists who inspired me to continue playing the bass during the times when I feel like giving up. I aspire to continue developing and improving my bass skills so that
one day I may also have an impact and maybe even inspire others to play. So here are some artists/bands who have inspired me to keep playing, and hopefully may inspire you too!
